Student Tracker Objectives

Parental Awareness

Advantage: Automatic notifications to parents about their child's attendance.

Benefit: Parents are kept informed about their child's attendance status, fostering a sense of involvement and allowing them to address attendance-related concerns promptly.

Accessibility and Flexibility

Objective: Make educational resources available to students anytime, anywhere.

Benefit: Online tutorials provide flexibility in learning, allowing students to access materials at their own pace and convenience, promoting self-directed learning.

Interactive Learning Experiences

Objective: Foster engagement through multimedia-rich tutorials.

Benefit: Incorporating multimedia elements such as videos, simulations, and interactive quizzes enhances student engagement, making learning more dynamic and effective.

Efficient Submission and Grading

Objective: Streamline the submission and grading process.

Benefit: Online submission features ease the workload for educators. Automated grading functionalities reduce administrative burdens and provide timely feedback to students.

Enhanced Teacher-Student Interaction

Objective: Facilitate communication between teachers and students.

Benefit: Direct communication channels enable educators to provide clarifications, guidance, and support to students in real-time.

Timely Communication

Objective: Ensure timely dissemination of important information.

Benefit: Notifications provide a quick and efficient way to communicate essential updates, announcements, and events, ensuring that stakeholders are promptly informed.

Payment Reminders

Objective: Remind parents of upcoming payment deadlines.

Benefit: Automated payment reminders through notifications ensure that parents are reminded of school fee payments, contributing to a smooth and timely financial process.
